"I built this class so you know what
I had to find out the long way."

I grew up without anyone to teach me about money — and the corporate world didn't slow down to explain it. From day one I was handed financial decisions that carried real weight: retirement accounts, investment options, benefits elections. The kind of things everyone around me seemed to already understand. I learned by asking questions, quietly Googling things I was embarrassed not to know, and piecing it together on my own. I figured it out — but I shouldn't have had to.

I spent the next 15+ years in corporate America — managing upward of $1 billion in revenue programs and training over 1,000 sales professionals how to communicate complex ideas simply and get people to take action. And somewhere along the way, I realized: that's exactly what financial education needs. Not more jargon. Not more charts. Just someone who can break it down in plain English and help you take the first step.

I built FLOW with Krystle because nobody built it for me. FLOW exists to help people navigate the most important topics around wealth and health — and to help you flow through life with awareness and understanding. Because when you understand both, everything changes.
